    PLOWING

    It loosen the soil,improve air and water flow and help mix nutrients

    PLANTING

    Healthy Bananas suckers are planted in rows,proper spacing is maintained(6-8 feet apart)

    03

    IRRIGATION

    Drip irrigation is commonly used for efficient water use

    04

    FERTILATION

    Organic or chemical fertliser are applied in stages for health growth
